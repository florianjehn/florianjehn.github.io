What I have been working on

Hi, I’m Florian,

this place is meant to give an overview of all the output my academic work has produced so far. In case you just stumbled across this. Here's a short intro of myself:

I see myself as an agreeable and curious person, who likes to explore the world in a playful manner. My main interests are climate change, existential risks, feminism, history, forecasting, and food security. After doing a bachelor, master, and doctorate in environmental science, I am currently working on global food security and societal collapse.
