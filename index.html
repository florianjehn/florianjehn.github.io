Academic Papers

To find an up-to-date list of my papers, visit Google Scholar. But here is a short selection of my favorite papers so far:

Food Trade Disruption After Global Catastrophes

Food trade is crucial for a large fraction of the world population. But what would happen to it if a large catastrophe hit, like a supervolcanic eruption or extreme space weather? It would decrease dramatically. This shows that we need to prepare better for such events, even if they are unlikely.

Seaweed as a Resilient Food Solution After a Nuclear War

In this paper, we looked at the question of whether seaweed can make a meaningful contribution in scenarios where Earth's temperature suddenly cools due to catastrophic events like a nuclear winter. Turns out, it can!

Focus of the IPCC Assessment Reports Has Shifted to Lower Temperatures

Chances are we will end up with a warming of 2°C or more. The problem is that the vast majority of our climate change research focuses on 2°C and below instead.
